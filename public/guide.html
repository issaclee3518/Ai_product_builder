User guide

A short guide for first-time users

Pick 2D or 3D from the toolbar, set color and stroke width, and start working. Below are the basics for each mode.

Common

  • Select 2D or 3D at the top.
  • Use the color picker and width slider to style strokes.
  • When done, use Download to save PNG, or Clear to reset the board.

2D whiteboard

  • Draw — Drag on the canvas to draw strokes.
  • QuickShape — Draw something like a circle, rectangle, triangle, or line, then hold still without releasing to snap it into a clean shape. Release immediately to keep the freehand stroke.
  • Eraser — Switch to the eraser tool and drag over strokes to remove them.

3D whiteboard

  • Place — Drag a shape from the left palette onto the canvas.
  • Select — Click a shape to select it. Click empty space or press Esc to deselect.
  • Transform — With a selection, use W move, E rotate, and R scale gizmos.
  • Camera — With nothing selected, move with WASD and Q/E, orbit with right-drag. Adjust speed with the toolbar slider.
  • Duplicate — In move (W) mode, hold Space while dragging to copy and move a shape.
  • Fill — Select a shape, then pick a color from the toolbar.

Tips

  • Use 2D for quick notes; 3D for spatial layout and structure.
  • Download important sketches often — board content lives in the browser session by default.
