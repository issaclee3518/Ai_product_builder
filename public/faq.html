Frequently asked questions

Good to know before you use the service

Do I need an account?

No. Use it right away with no sign-up. Open the whiteboard in your browser and start drawing.

Where is my work saved?

The current version runs in your browser — there is no cloud sync or account storage. To keep your work, use Download in the toolbar to save a PNG file.

How does QuickShape work?

In 2D mode, draw something shape-like, then hold still without releasing to snap it into a line, circle, ellipse, rectangle, triangle, and more. Release immediately to keep the freehand stroke. See the guide for details.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Touch drawing and basic controls work on mobile. 3D gizmo and camera controls are easier with a keyboard.

Are ads on the site normal?

Ads such as Google AdSense may appear to support the service. See the privacy policy for ads and cookies.

Found a bug or have a suggestion?

Email issaclee6320@gmail.com with what happened. Include your browser and whether you were in 2D or 3D mode for faster help.

Is it free?

Core features on the web are free to use. Ads may help cover operating costs.
