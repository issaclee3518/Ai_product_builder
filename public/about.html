About Memo Whiteboard

issac.store · Browser-based 2D / 3D sketch tool

Memo Whiteboard lets you sketch ideas quickly in a web browser — no install or account required. Switch between a flat 2D canvas and a 3D placement mode for everything from quick notes to simple shape layouts.

Why we built it

For capturing structure, screen layouts, and simple diagrams in the moment — during meetings or when an idea strikes. Open one browser tab, draw lines, drop shapes, and add color without launching a heavy design app.

Features

  • 2D whiteboard — Draw freely; hold to snap strokes into lines, circles, rectangles, triangles, and more with QuickShape.
  • 3D whiteboard — Place shapes and move, rotate, scale, and navigate with the camera.
  • Color & stroke width — Adjust line color and thickness from the toolbar.
  • Save as image — Download your board as PNG.

Who it’s for

  • Students and developers who need fast sketches and notes
  • Anyone roughing out UI wireframes or structure in a meeting
  • People who want a lightweight, shareable board with no install
