What can you do?

  • 2D whiteboard — Draw freely; hold briefly to snap strokes into lines, circles, rectangles, and triangles with QuickShape.
  • Select & move shapes — Click a stroke outline to select, drag to move, copy, paste, or delete.
  • 3D whiteboard — Place shapes, move, rotate, and scale them while exploring the scene with the camera.
  • Save as image — Download your board as PNG to share or keep.

Great for

Roughing out structure before opening a heavy design tool, quick notes in class or meetings, and switching between 2D and 3D to organize ideas. Open instantly with no account — save only what you need as an image.

Getting started

  1. Click Open whiteboard to launch the board.
  2. Choose 2D or 3D mode from the toolbar.
  3. Draw and place shapes, then save as an image.
